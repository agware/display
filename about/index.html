About

Interactive Formulas was developed by Alex Ware as part of CSIRO’s 2016/2017 Vacation Scholarship program.

The goal of these animations is not to teach physics concepts but instead to act as teaching aids. The idea is that by allowing students to play around with formulas and instantly receive visual feedback, the underlying concepts are made more accessible. This allows students to develop a deeper and more intuitive understanding of what is going on behind the numbers.
